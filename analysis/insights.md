# Marketplace Product Insights

## Executive Summary

This analysis explores an Etsy-like marketplace focusing on activation, repeat behavior, and short-term retention.

The primary issue identified is weak retention despite strong initial activation.

---

## Key Metrics

- Total Revenue: 730
- Activation Rate: 80%
- Repeat Rate: 25%
- Average Orders per User: 1.25
- D7 Retention: 0%

---

## Key Findings

### 1. Strong Activation

80% of users complete at least one purchase.
Onboarding appears effective.

### 2. Weak Repeat Behavior

Only 25% of users place more than one order.
Retention is the main growth bottleneck.

### 3. No Short-Term Retention

D7 retention is 0%, indicating no users return within 7 days.
This suggests lack of habit formation.

### 4. Category Performance

Electronics generates the majority of revenue.
Home category contributes the least.

---

## Recommendations

1. Introduce post-purchase engagement strategies.
2. Implement re-engagement campaigns within 7 days.
3. Incentivize second purchase behavior.
4. Evaluate weak-performing categories.

---

## Strategic Focus

Shift focus from acquisition to retention optimization.
